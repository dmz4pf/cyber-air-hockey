## Known Visual Quirks

### Safari
- Particle trails flicker (WebKit blend mode)
- Slight stutter on scroll (layout repaint)
- Fix: Use `will-change: transform` on trails

### Firefox
- Additive blending darker than Chrome
- Fix: Increase particle opacity to 0.9

### Mobile Safari
- 30fps cap on animations
- Particle count auto-reduced to 1000
- No cursor trails (touch only)
